My Scientific Background

As researcher I study past and present societies.

I worked on themes like water management and climate change in Antiquity, past and present nomadic agency in nomad-sedentary interaction or female agency in involuntary resettlement with various international projects in Sudan, Egypt, Qatar, the United Kingdom, Poland and Germany. My clients include the British Museum, the University College London in London and in Qatar, Michigan University, the German Archaeological Institute, Humboldt University, Museum of London Archaeology. I concluded my projects with presentations and publication.
